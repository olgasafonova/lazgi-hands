## Key Observations

### Wrist Characteristics
- Wrists are rarely straight
- Bent at 30-45 degree angles
- Creates the characteristic "broken" look

### Finger Positions
- Fingers often slightly curved
- Not fully extended, not fully closed
- Middle and ring fingers often together
- Index and pinky more separated

### Arm Lines
- Arms never fully locked at elbow
- Soft curves throughout
- One arm higher, one lower (asymmetry)
